Venue and logistics

How to get to the conference.

Venue

Palazzo Franchetti

The conference will be held at Palazzo Franchetti of the Venetian Institute of Science, Letters and Arts. The address of the venue is:

Campo S. Stefano, 2945
30124, San Marco, Venice
Telephone: +39 041 2407711

The registration desk opens Tuesday 9th at 8:45 am; when you check in to the conference we will give you a badge and the workshop material.


We will not print the program and other information, but they will be always available at the conference website.

How to get to Venice

From the airport

The "Marco Polo" Venice airport is on the mainland, around 5km north of Venice. The city can be easily reached by water bus, shuttle bus or taxi. More information available on the airport website.

The city can also be reached easily from the "A. Canova" Treviso airport by bus. More information available on the airport website.

Accomodation

We strongly suggest to reserve your accommodation at Don Orione or to Domus Ciliota guest house, both very close to the conference site. To book a room write directly to info@donorione-venezia.it or to info@ciliota.it
